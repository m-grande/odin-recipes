Description

Homemade lasagna is a classic Italian-American dish made with layers of pasta, a rich meat sauce (often called rag√π or bolognese), a creamy cheese filling, and plenty of shredded cheese, all baked until bubbly and golden brown. It's a comforting and hearty meal, perfect for family dinners or gatherings.

Ingridients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef and/or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Canned crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and/or tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Egg
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Seasonings: salt, black pepper, Italian seasoning, basil, oregano, fennel seeds (optional), red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce
  2. Prepare the Cheese Filling
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles (if using regular noodles)
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake the Lasagna
  6. Rest and Serve
